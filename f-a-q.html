Frequently Asked Questions

If you have questions about our robot, come here! If you have a question not on this list, ask us during a meet, or visit the contact page.
What can our autonomous do?
Our robot can detect the randomization, and park in the corresponding spot, on either side of the play field.
What can we do in TeleOp?
In TeleOp, we can pick up and place cones in the low, and medium junctions, as well as place the beacon in endgame.
What is a strategy we like to do?
We like to stack a bunch of cones on medium and/or low junctions, and have the other team on the alliance make a circuit with ground junctions. In endgame, we can place a beacon on a medium or low junction, then park to end the game.
