Bumblebee

Our robot is designed to get points from an arm, and move around freely because of it's small size.
The Robot The robot can lift cones off the ground, and has an autonomous that can park when looking at the cone in front of it.
The Arm, and Camera The camera helps with our autonomous because it will detect the cone and decide what to do depending on what it sees, The arm can reach the low, and middle junctions.
